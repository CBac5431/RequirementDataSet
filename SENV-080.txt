SENV-080
H/P
The contractor shall be responsible for performing radiation analyses
as required using the nominal mission scenario and taking into
account the data from the Herschel L2 Radiation Environment
document (AD5-1). The in-orbit case with a beginning of mission in
February 2007 shall be taken into account.
In particular, the figures related to the Silicon Solar cells, Gallium
Arsenide cells, single dose/depth, non-ionising dose/depth and raw
particle spectra shall be taken into account.
