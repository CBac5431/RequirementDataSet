SENV-100
H/P
Components shall be qualified (either based on existing or new test
data) to withstand the doses predicted according to SENV-085.
Radiation testing shall be included in the lot acceptance testing, if the
margin is small and if the variation of radiation resistance between lots
is large or insufficiently known. An exception to this requirement is the
solar cells, which shall be qualified according to applicable documents
(AD6-8/1 and AD6-8/2).
