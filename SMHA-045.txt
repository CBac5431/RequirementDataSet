SMHA-045
H/P
Physical separation along common lines of the categories listed below
(power, signals and lines for the mechanisms, if applicable) shall be
retained between these categories up to and inclusive of the module
interface connectors. Exceptions can be only the routing of harnesses
down to connectors in the satellite separation plane.
- Category 1: all supply lines from power sources to users
- Category 2: digital signals
- Category 3: lines for the mechanisms/pyrotechnics
- Category 4: sensitive analogue signals.
