SFUN-010
H
The Herschel Payload Module shall:
- Provide the necessary interfaces (mechanical, optical and
electrical) with the Agency’s provided Telescope and other
elements of the spacecraft.
- Accommodate the focal plane units (FPU) of the instruments, the
Local Oscillator Unit (LOU) of HIFI and the Buffer Amplifier Unit
(BOLA) of PACS in accordance with the requirements and interface
specification of the IID’s Part B (AD4-2, AD4-3, AD4-4)
- Provide to the instruments FPU's, the required thermal
environment, through a cryogenic subsystem
