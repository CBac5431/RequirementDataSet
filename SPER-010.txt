SPER-010
P
For the Planck mission , the Planck spacecraft shall have a nominal
lifetime of at least 21 months from launch till the end of mission .
This duration shall allow two full sky surveys ( with a coverage of at
least 95% of the full sky) at the operational Lissajous orbit around L-2
, and includes an allocation of 6 months for the transfer to the L-2
Lissajous orbit.
