SMCD-170
H/P
At all times, the CDMS shall record key on board events in a
Spacecraft Event Log (SEL) stored in the on-board Data Storage.
Key events include at least:
i. H/W and S/W autonomous actions
ii. H/W and S/W failures
iii. H/W and S/W reconfigurations
iv. S/W start-up and shutdown
v. Procedure start-up and completion
vi. Recording of the major Mission Timeline (MTL) events
Recording of the major manual actions (initiated during DTCP), etc
