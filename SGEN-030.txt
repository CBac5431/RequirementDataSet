SGEN-030
H/P
Each spacecraft shall be configured in a modular way, with:
- each respective Service Module (SVM), which provides the
spacecraft subsystems working in a normal temperature
environment,
- the Herschel Payload Module (PLM), including the cold focal plane
units of the scientific instruments and the cryogenic subsystem as
well as some warm payload equipment,
- the Herschel Telescope,
- the Planck Payload Module consisting of the telescope with the
instruments and all necessary equipment for a separate integration.
